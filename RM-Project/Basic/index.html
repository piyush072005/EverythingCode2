FAQ

Quick answers

Does checking eligibility affect my credit?

No. We run a soft inquiry for pre-approval. A hard pull happens only if you accept and proceed with funding.

How fast can I get funded?

Personal and business working-capital loans are often funded within 24 hours after document verification. Equipment and larger loans may take 2-5 days.

Can I repay early?

Yes. There are no prepayment penalties on eligible loan types. Early repayment can reduce total interest paid.
